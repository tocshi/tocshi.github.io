Photoshop Projects


Photoshop Express Edits

This project was made using the free online service Photoshop Express. I was assigned to modify different pictures using the various effect and filter tools that Photoshop Express has to offer. The effects were simple on their own, so I decided to combine different ones for each of my pictures, creating astounding effects.


Undertale Magazine Covers

These two magazine covers are based off of one of my favourite RPGs, Undertale. They were put together in Adobe Photoshop. I utilized multiple layers, magic wand, guides, and many other designing tools to collage these magazine covers together. I have created many similar covers before, so these magazine covers were a breeze to create.
